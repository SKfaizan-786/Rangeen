Revolutionizing SAR Image Colourization with Deep Learning

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) images provide valuable data in all weather conditions. With Deep Learning, we bring colors and life to SAR images, enhancing visual understanding and interpretation.

Overview of SAR Image Colorization

Problem Statement

SAR images are difficult to interpret in their original grayscale format, limiting their utility in fields like geology and disaster management.

Solution Overview

We utilize a deep learning approach (Pix2Pix or cGAN) for colorizing these images, enhancing their usefulness in spaceborne applications.

Use Cases and Applications

Real-world applications include disaster management, geological mapping, and environmental monitoring. Colorized SAR images significantly improve interpretation and analysis.
